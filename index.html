Pomodoro Study Timer

Ready

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. Use this timer to improve your focus and productivity during study sessions.

25:00

Custom Settings

Pomodoro Technique Tips

  • Choose a task you want to accomplish
  • Set the timer for 25 minutes and focus solely on that task
  • When the timer rings, take a 5-minute break
  • After 4 pomodoros, take a longer break (15-30 minutes)
  • Use breaks to stretch, hydrate, or rest your eyes
  • Track your progress to see how many pomodoros you complete daily
